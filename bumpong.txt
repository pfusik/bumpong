*** BUMPONG ***
Game for two players

First presented at WAP-NIAK 2012
Warsaw, Poland

Requires: 8-bit Atari (400/800/XL/XE)
PAL version with at least 48k RAM
and a good GTIA chip
Recommended: two joysticks
