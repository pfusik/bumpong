*** BUMPONG ***
Game for two players
Play classic pong with
the bump mapping effect!

First presented at WAP-NIAK 2012
Warsaw, Poland

Requires: 8-bit Atari (400/800/XL/XE)
with 48k RAM and a good GTIA chip
Game is PAL/NTSC compatible
Recommended: two joysticks

Design and programming
by Piotr '0xF' Fusik <fox@scene.pl>
